About this project

This project is aimed to illustrate how commuters in New York City used Citi Bikes as one of their transportation options. Datum are filtered to focus on rush hours(7am-9am) in weekdays morning and trips from bikers with annual pass.

Red "startpoint" are locations where commuters picked up the bikes and green "endpoint" are those where commuters brought bikes back.

Map data courtesy of Mapbox Studio, Citi Bike and New York City DOT
